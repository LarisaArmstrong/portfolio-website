Larisa Armstrong
atist's statement
Larisa Armstrong (born in 1996) is a South African contemporary UI and UX designer who also focuses on multimedia including motion graphics, sound design and film editing. Her approach to this is very raw and often communicates statements that arouse curiosity. Her design work has a simple, yet bold aesthetic to it. When appropriate, she brings a human touch to her work through the use of courageous and spirited elements.

Larisa is also a photographer, who focuses on methodically documenting inhabitants around South Africa. For her current project, namely 'Paroikeo', she explores the human behaviour which interlinks the spaces that are invaded by using an observation and study as part of her research for the notion of ‘home’. She intends on collecting answers to certain questions within her surroundings to define a more honest approach to the subjects she encounters. Overall, she creates arrangements of ideas that we can all respond to which delivers a personal narrative.
